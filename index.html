Many Styles of Yoga to Suit Everyone

From gentle restorative flows to powerful vinyasa, we offer classes for every body and every level.

Hatha Yoga

A gentle introduction to the most basic yoga postures. Perfect for beginners seeking balance.

Vinyasa Yoga

A dynamic practice linking breath and movement. Build heat, strength and flexibility.

Vinyasa Flow

Flow from pose to pose in a continuous sequence. Perfect for those who love movement.

Mullo Yoga

Focus on grounding poses that connect you to the earth. Develop a strong foundation.

Restorative Yoga

Creative sequences with fluid transitions. Express yourself through mindful movement.

Yin Yoga

Long held poses combined with breathwork. Release tension and find deep calm.

Our studio was founded with a simple belief: yoga should be accessible, inclusive, and transformative for everyone. Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening a lifelong practice, you belong here.

We offer over 30 classes per week across all styles and levels. Our certified instructors bring warmth, expertise and genuine care to every session. We create a space where you can leave the outside world behind and reconnect with yourself.

From sunrise flows to evening restorative sessions, our schedule is designed to fit your life. Join our community and discover what's possible when mind, body and breath align.
